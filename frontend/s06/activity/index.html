Character Profile

SoundWave

Who is Sound Wave?

Soundwave is the Decepticons' cold, analytical Communications Officer and Megatron’s most loyal lieutenant, defined by his stoic "Cassel-robot" persona and ability to monitor all transmissions. Originally a gladiator or data clerk in various origins, he rose to power by using blackmail and surveillance to become indispensable to the Decepticon cause. His iconic design features a blue and white microcassette recorder chest—serving as a hangar for his "Deployer" minions like Laserbeak and Ravage—and a faceplate that obscures all emotion, punctuated by his synthesized, monotone voice.
