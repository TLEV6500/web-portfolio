2 Top Travel Destinations 🛫

Thailand

Thailand is a tropical paradise where you can live like royalty for 120 a day, provided you survive the chaotic tuk-tuk rides and don't accidentally insult someone while trying to order spicy papaya salad. It’s the "Land of Smiles" because everyone is perpetually happy, or perhaps they are just laughing at your failed attempts to pronounce the five different tones of Thai.

Paris

Paris is a magical, crowded city where you can fall in love with a croissant, get lost in a museum, and accidentally pay ($20) for a coffee near the Eiffel Tower. It’s essentially a stunningly beautiful, chaotic romantic comedy where the locals pretend not to understand your French while walking faster than you ever thought possible.

Top 3 essentials to bring ✅

Money💵

If you got the money to travel in the first place, it will be a waste if you don't have enough money to enjoy your stay.

Important Documents📃

Bring the necessary documents to travel back and forth, and protect them with your life.

Your Brain🧠

Having this will help you make the most of your travel. It will keep you safe, it will help you with your expenses, and it will allow you to survive the entire journey.
